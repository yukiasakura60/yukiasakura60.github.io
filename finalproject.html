Yuki Asakura's Final Project Sketch

This is my final project sketch: Smart Port. It is a plant pot with an LED strip that lets users know when to water their plants. It also has an additional bluetooth speaker that blends seemlessly around the pot.


Bill of Materials

Main Components

  • Arduino Pro Mini 328 - 5V/16MHz
  • SparkFun Soil Moisture Sensor
  • Piezo Speaker - PC Mount 12mm 2.048kHz
  • 9V Alkaline Battery
  • Adafruit Bluefruit LE UART Friend - Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)
  • Flexibe LED Strip Lights

Secondary Components

  • Resistors
  • Breadboard
  • Wires
